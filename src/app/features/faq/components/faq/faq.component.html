FAQ

What are main functions of this application?

Gifts Helper provides 2 main functionality for you

  1. Create and manage your own christmas whish list in the "Wish List" section
  2. Search for your friend or family member wish list by using their email in the "Profiles" section
  3. Assign one or more items from other person wish list as an item you would like to byu for her/him. You can see this assigned items in "Shopping List" section
What if I buy one of the items from my shopping list?

You can mark every item as "bought" from the item menu. If someone else buys this item first, you will get a warning and won't be able to mark it as bought

Searching for a person each time I want to see his or her list is tedious

No problem! You can mark each person as observed and link to the profile will always appear at the top of "Profiles" section

I saw some bugs in the app or just want to propose some improvements

You can click a button with exclamation mark in the header and fill a small form with your message
